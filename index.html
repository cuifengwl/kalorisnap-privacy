KaloriSnap Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

What we collect

KaloriSnap stores your profile (age, sex, height, weight, activity level, goal) and your meal history locally on your device. Food photos you scan are sent to our AI provider solely to analyze the food, and are not used to identify you.

How photos are processed

When you scan a meal, the photo is transmitted securely (HTTPS) to an AI image analysis service to estimate nutritional content. Photos are processed transiently for analysis and are not used for advertising.

What we do NOT do

We do not sell your personal data. We do not show third-party ads. We do not require an account, and we do not collect your name, email, or contacts.

Data retention & deletion

Your profile and meal history live on your device. You can permanently delete all data at any time via Settings → Delete all my data. Deleting the app also removes all locally stored data.

Subscriptions

Payments are handled entirely by Apple. We never see or store your payment details.

Children

KaloriSnap is not intended for children under 13.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Contact us at support@kalorisnap.app.

KaloriSnap 隐私政策

最后更新：2026 年 7 月

我们收集什么

KaloriSnap 将你的个人档案（年龄、性别、身高、体重、活动量、目标）和餐食记录存储在你的设备本地。你扫描的食物照片仅会发送给 AI 服务商用于分析食物，不会用于识别你的身份。

照片如何处理

当你扫描餐食时，照片会通过加密连接（HTTPS）传输至 AI 图像分析服务以估算营养成分。照片仅在分析过程中临时处理，不会用于广告。

我们不会做什么

我们不出售你的个人数据，不展示第三方广告，不要求注册账号，也不收集你的姓名、邮箱或通讯录。

数据保留与删除

你的档案和餐食记录保存在你的设备上。你可以随时通过「设置 → 删除我的所有数据」永久删除全部数据。卸载 App 也会删除所有本地数据。

订阅

付款完全由 Apple 处理，我们不会看到或存储你的付款信息。

儿童

KaloriSnap 不适用于 13 岁以下儿童。

联系我们

如有隐私相关问题，请联系 support@kalorisnap.app。
